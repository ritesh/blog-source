Geared bikes

Posted on August 10, 2013 in bikes

Got a sweet road bike off Gumtree. I thought the gears weren’t working, having never ridden a geared bike before. Me: “The gears on this bike don’t work” Bike shop guy: “Have you tried shifting these levers right here?” Me: “Those are brakes, right?” Bike shop guy: “How have you been shifting gears until this point?” Me: “I’ll show myself out, thanks” Laughter in the background as I walk out of the bike shop.
